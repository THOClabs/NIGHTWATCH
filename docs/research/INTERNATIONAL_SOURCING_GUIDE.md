# NIGHTWATCH International Sourcing Guide
## Multi-Language Component Research & Global Vendor Directory

*Making open-source astronomy truly open to the world*

---

## Purpose

This guide extends NIGHTWATCH sourcing research to a global audience. Astronomical equipment markets vary significantly by region—some components are easier to find in Europe, others in Asia or the Americas. By documenting vendors, forums, and search terms in multiple languages, we enable contributors and builders worldwide to source components locally, reducing shipping costs and import complications.

## 6. Import/Export Considerations

### Optical Instruments Import Duties

| Region | Duty Rate | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **USA** | 0% - 6.6% | Telescopes often 0% (HTS 9005) |
| **EU** | 0% - 4.2% | Varies by specific item |
| **UK** | 0% - 4.2% | Post-Brexit, similar to EU |
| **Australia** | 0% - 5% | Educational/scientific may be exempt |
| **Canada** | 0% - 6% | Scientific instruments often reduced |
| **Japan** | 0% | Most optical instruments duty-free |
| **China** | 8% - 20% | Varies significantly |

### Shipping Carriers for Telescope Equipment

| Carrier | Best For | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **FedEx International** | USA ↔ Europe | Good for fragile items |
| **DHL Express** | Global | Excellent tracking, fast |
| **UPS Worldwide** | North America | Good domestic + Canada |
| **USPS Priority International** | Budget shipping | Slower, less tracking |
| **EMS (national posts)** | Asia ↔ anywhere | Often cheapest for Asia |
| **SF Express** | China origin | Good for China shipments |
| **Yamato Transport** | Japan origin | Excellent for Japan |

### Packing Recommendations

- Always double-box telescopes with 3+ inches of padding
- Declare accurate values for customs (under-declaring risks seizure)
- Include commercial invoice outside the box
- Mark "FRAGILE - OPTICAL INSTRUMENT" in destination language
- Consider insurance for items over $500

## 7. Currency & Payment Methods

### International Payment Options

| Method | Best For | Fees | Notes |
|--------|----------|------|-------|
| **PayPal** | Global | 2-4% | Good buyer protection |
| **Wise (TransferWise)** | Bank transfers | 0.5-1% | Best exchange rates |
| **Credit Card** | Most vendors | 1-3% forex | Check foreign transaction fees |
| **Alipay** | China vendors | Low | Required for Taobao |
| **WeChat Pay** | China vendors | Low | Common in China |
| **PayPay** | Japan vendors | Low | Common in Japan |
| **SEPA Transfer** | EU vendors | Free/low | Within Eurozone |

### Currency Conversion Tips

- **Always pay in local currency** - Avoid vendor conversion (Dynamic Currency Conversion)
- **Check your bank's forex fees** - Some cards charge 3%+
- **Use Wise/Revolut for better rates** - Often 0.5% vs bank 2-3%
- **Large purchases**: Wire transfer may be cheaper than PayPal
